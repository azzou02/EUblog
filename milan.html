milan record

Day 16 (5/28) - Train Grind + Armani

Train ride to Milan! No one was left behind and we got all luggages to fit in the compartments. It was a work session for (most) of us :D
The Armani exhibition was gorgeous! I (Michelle) would say it's on par with Dior. There's this sense of elegance and intricate details that really stood out to me. I loved his attention to detail where the exbition lighting reflecting against the marble walls reflect the theme of the dresses.

Day 17 (5/29) - D House + Duomo

This was one of my (Alison) favorite visits of this trip - D House Academy. I was in awe of the technology they used to create garments - polyjet printing, real-time thread coloring, thermo welding, ultrasonic welding, and more. It help me understand scale of invovlement technology has in fashion, and the infinite creative possibilities.
After our visit, we went to see the Duomo as well as the infamous galleria. Both are beautiful pieces of work! We also went to this calzone store that is super famous ( had to wait in line, but it went super fast) and I was pleasently surprised by the price and flavor.

Day 18 (5/30) - Presentation + Farewell Dinner

Our final dinner! It was great getting to learn everything about fashion, business, and sustainability and meeting eveyone in the class. The Dior museum was especially captivating. Best maymester ever :D
A funny original experience in the Metro to close off the trip.
